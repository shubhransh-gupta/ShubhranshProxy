Data filtering

How we filter and organize your traffic

Raw packet dumps are useless. ShubhranshProxy transforms a firehose of network data into structured, actionable intelligence through three layers of filtering:

Domain intelligence

Traffic is automatically grouped by hostname. Each domain expands to reveal its API endpoints — so you see api.example.com/auth/login not a flat list of 4,000 rows. Pin domains you care about; they persist across sessions until you remove them.

Device segmentation

Every request is tagged with its source: Safari on Mac, your iPhone at 192.168.0.12, an Android emulator. Filter by device to isolate mobile-only traffic without noise from your desktop.

Selective SSL rules

Unlike tools that decrypt everything and break your browser, ShubhranshProxy defaults to passthrough. Add per-host rules like *.api.myapp.io and only those connections get decrypted. Pages load normally; your targets don't.

Rewrite & Map Local

Swap responses with local files via Map Local, redirect endpoints with Map Remote, or inject breakpoints to pause and edit requests mid-flight before they reach the server.

Throttle simulation

Simulate 3G, Edge, or custom latency profiles to test how your app behaves on slow networks — without leaving your desk or touching device settings.

In-memory sessions

Captured data lives entirely in RAM. Quit the app and it's gone — no sensitive traffic written to disk, no cleanup required. Privacy by architecture, not policy.
