About ShubhranshProxy

After working with Charles Proxy and Proxyman — and even building earlier proxy experiments like Charles_Alternate — I wanted a native macOS tool that gave developers the full debugging toolkit without a paid license or subscription.

ShubhranshProxy captures HTTP(S) traffic from your Mac, iPhone, and Android on the same Wi‑Fi, with SSL decryption, Map Local, breakpoints, throttling, and HAR export — 100% free, forever.
