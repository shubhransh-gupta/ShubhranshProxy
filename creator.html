About ShubhranshProxy

After years of working with legacy proxy tools — and building earlier experiments like Charles_Alternate — I set out to build a native macOS debugger that gives developers the full toolkit without a paid license or subscription.

ShubhranshProxy captures HTTP(S) traffic from your Mac, iPhone, and Android on the same Wi‑Fi, with SSL decryption, Map Local, breakpoints, throttling, and HAR export — 100% free, forever.
